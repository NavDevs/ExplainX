# Privacy Policy for ExplainX

**Effective Date: April 18, 2026**

ExplainX ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how it handles information when you use the ExplainX Chrome Extension.

## 1. Information We Collect
ExplainX is designed with a "Privacy First" approach. 
- **User Content**: We only process the text you explicitly highlight or messages you type into the chat interface.
- **No Personal Data**: We do not collect names, email addresses, browsing history (outside of highlighted text), or sensitive personal information.

## 2. How Information is Used
The information collected is strictly used for the following:
- Providing AI-generated explanations via the selected AI provider (Google Gemini API).
- Rendering these explanations within the extension's user interface.

## 3. Data Storage
- **Local Storage**: Your chat history and settings are stored locally on your own computer using the `chrome.storage.local` API. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly interact with the AI features.
- **Third-Party Processing**: Highlighted text and chat messages are sent to Google Gemini for the sole purpose of generating explanations. Please refer to [Google's AI Privacy Policy](https://ai.google.com/privacy/) for details on how they handle data.

## 4. Third-Party Services
ExplainX uses the following third-party APIs:
- **Google Gemini API**: For AI-powered text analysis and response generation.

## 5. User Control
You can clear your chat history at any time using the "Clear Chat" button within the extension UI. Uninstalling the extension will remove all locally stored data.

## 6. Access and Permissions
ExplainX requires the following permissions to function:
- `storage`: To save your chat history and theme preferences.
- `activeTab`: To read the text you've highlighted on the current page.
- `scripting`: To inject the explanation sidebar into the host page.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## 8. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the official repository or project documentation.
